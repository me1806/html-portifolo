Movie Ranking

My 3 best movies of this year

Fight Club

It is based on the 1996 novel by Chuck Palahniuk. Norton plays the unnamed narrator, who is discontented with his white-collar job. He forms a "fight club" with soap salesman Tyler Durden (Pitt), and becomes embroiled in a relationship with an impoverished but beguilingly attractive woman, Marla Singer (Bonham Carter).

Dead poet's society

The film, starring Robin Williams, is set in 1959 at a fictional elite boarding school called Welton Academy, and tells the story of an English teacher who inspires his students through his teaching of poetry.

Hacksaw ridge

Hacksaw Ridge" is an intense and gripping war drama that tells the inspiring true story of Desmond Doss, a conscientious objector who served as a medic during World War II. The film beautifully portrays Doss's unwavering courage and commitment to his beliefs in the face of unimaginable adversity.
